Digital Ricoeur: Ricoeur's Social Network

This will serve as an interactive presentation of Ricoeur's Social Network, based on the frequency at which Ricoeur refers to other thinkers.
We currently have an interactive bar graph, using a CSV file with made-up data.
The CSV file comprises of four columns, Thinker , Frequency , Year , and Title of Ricoeur's publication.
